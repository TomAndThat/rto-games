# The Catfish Game

A game of dating and deception

## Specs

**Players**: 3 - 20

## Game Summary

Through a series of rounds, players will complete their dating profiles.

At the same time, they will attempt to complete other players' dating profiles - trying to pass themselves off as the genuine article.

After each round, players will vote on who they think is the genuine article. If you are able to correctly identify the real person, you get a point, but if you get it wrong, the point goes to the catfish.

## Game Structure

1. **Lobby** - players waiting to start the game

2. **Profile Picture** - players draw their own profile pictures. There are no attempts at impersonation during this round

3. **Text Prompt** - players answer a prompt with text-based response
   - players answer their own prompt
   - players answer another player's prompt
   - players answer a third player's prompt

4. **Voting** - players cast their votes on the previous round, trying to identify the real player and avoid the catfish

5. **Image Prompt** - players answer a prompt with image-based response
   - players answer their own prompt
   - players answer another player's prompt
   - players answer a third player's prompt

6. **Voting** - players cast their votes on the previous round, trying to identify the real player and avoid the catfish

7. **Text Prompt** - players answer a prompt with text-based response
   - players answer their own prompt
   - players answer another player's prompt
   - players answer a third player's prompt

8. **Voting** - players cast their votes on the previous round, trying to identify the real player and avoid the catfish

9. **Image Prompt** - players answer a prompt with image-based response
   - players answer their own prompt
   - players answer another player's prompt
   - players answer a third player's prompt

10. **Voting** - players cast their votes on the previous round, trying to identify the real player and avoid the catfish
